Energy Programs Available

Choose from 12 proven programs designed to save you money

wb_sunny

Home Solar Ownership Program

Transform your home into a personal power station with $0 upfront cost. Own your solar system outright, lower your monthly bills, and gain the ability to sell excess energy back to the grid. No leasing — full ownership for maximum long-term savings.

schedule

Smart Time-of-Use Plan

Pay less by shifting energy use to off-peak hours. This flexible plan adjusts rates based on time of day and season, letting you save money without sacrificing comfort.

analytics

Flat Rate Protection Plan

Lock in one predictable monthly bill based on your usage history — no seasonal spikes or unexpected surprises. Save 10–30% compared to standard variable rates.

thermostat

Peak Demand Saver Program

For homes with qualifying smart thermostats, this program optimizes your air conditioning during peak demand times to reduce strain on the grid while lowering your bill.

family_restroom

Family Energy Discount (FERA)

Households slightly above standard income limits can still qualify for up to 18% off monthly electricity bills. Designed to keep energy affordable for working families.

account_balance_wallet

Low-Income Energy Assistance (CARE)

Eligible households can save 30–35% on electric bills and 20% on natural gas bills through this statewide program.

bolt

Custom Rate Optimization

Switch to a rate plan that matches your lifestyle — whether you want the lowest possible per-kWh cost or a billing cycle that aligns with your budget.

home

Attic Efficiency Rebate

Receive rebates for installing high-performance attic insulation. Reduce heat loss, improve year-round comfort, and lower heating/cooling costs.

eco

California Clean Energy Standard

Join the movement toward renewable power. This program supports California's goal of generating a larger share of electricity from wind, solar, and other clean sources.

credit_card

Energy Upgrade Financing (REEL-in)

Special financing for homeowners and renters to complete energy-saving home improvements. Simple approval process with competitive rates.

science

Innovation & Technology Grant

A statewide network connecting innovators with funding, training, and partnerships to bring new clean-energy solutions to market.

trending_up

Clean Energy Investment Fund (EPIC)

Funding for clean-energy projects that improve efficiency and expand renewable technology in underserved communities.
